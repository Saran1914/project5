My Favourite Book - Your Time will come

The Book YOUR TIME WILL COME :A Story of Second Chances in Life When We Fall Hard is written

by Saranya Umakanthan,a Software Engineer.This is a charming love novel starring Siddharth and Shanaya.

A brilliantly crafted tale about second chances when life goes wrong! How does one react when the person

they love is married to another? What occurs when the person you love remains infatuated with his ex? These

themes are the story's focal point.Shanaya, Siddharth, and Mishti are portrayed in a remarkably realistic manner.

The actions and consequences that led to their split or reconciliation are so realistic. We have certainly

witnessed such occurrences around us. There are additional characters in this novel who assist the protagonist.


Each chapter begins with an insightful quotation. There are a few places where I felt the plot was bogged down by

too much description, but overall, the story runs well.Other than that, it was an enjoyable read. Numerous emotions

permeate the plot, including love, rejection, sympathy, pity, etc. There is also evidence of a new friendship,

the loss of family members, and trust concerns.

My Thoughts about the Book :

My favourite aspect of this book is because it begins with the chapter named The End and finishes with The Beginning!

The title of the book is so appropriate and relevant to the plot. The book's cover is intriguing.If you enjoy the Romance
